# Visual Quality Rules

Use this as the mandatory visual gate for Xiaohongshu covers, carousel cards, title cards, subtitle styles, and video visual packages.

## Core Standard

The visual must look publishable on a phone feed. It should be readable, intentional, and specific to the content.

## Checks

- The main subject is clear at first glance.
- The cover headline is readable in a Xiaohongshu feed thumbnail.
- Text does not cover the face, product, key screenshot, or important visual detail.
- The design has a clear hierarchy: main title, optional subtitle, supporting visual.
- Colors do not feel muddy, cheap, or randomly chosen.
- Fonts are consistent and not over-decorated.
- There are no obvious AI-image artifacts in hands, faces, text, logos, or object edges.
- The image does not look like a generic stock graphic unless that is intentional.
- Carousel pages do not cram caption paragraphs onto the image.
- Video captions do not block the subject or important action.

## Preferred Visual Direction

- Use real footage/photos when the content depends on authenticity.
- Use generated backgrounds only when they support the topic and do not fake evidence.
- For educational or tool-related posts, prioritize clarity over decoration.
- For personal/vlog posts, preserve atmosphere and real details.
- For covers, prefer one strong visual idea over many small elements.

## Required Before Delivery

When producing visual files, inspect the final output before calling it done. If browser/canvas/screenshot tools are available, use them. If not, describe the remaining uncertainty and ask for a visual review.
